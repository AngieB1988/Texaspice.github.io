We are Texas Spice Company

Texas Spice Company, Inc., founded in 1985, is a custom spice blending company and spice supplier to the Industrial Food Producers and a Food Service Distributor. Whether it’s your proprietary formula or one of our own recipes, we will work with you to produce the exact flavor and measurement your company is looking for.

Since 1985, we have been spicing up the world’s best-loved foods. At Texas Spice Company we draw on a wealth of global resources which allows us access to exceptional raw materials. Thus, we can offer the Best selection of Spices at Extremely competitive prices. In fact, we are direct importers and suppliers with direct distributions covering all of North America. Ensuring the quality of our products is a high priority for Texas Spice Company, Inc.; so for the majority, we manage the entire process from purchasing and blending through customer delivery. Today, we also produce a full line of Specialty Products Texas Spice Company provides top-notch Award Winning Custom Blending for Wholesale and Retail; along with our Coffee & Tea Programs and great customer support – we have a combination that can’t be beat!
